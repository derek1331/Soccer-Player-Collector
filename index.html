Soccer Player Collecter
Instructions

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.


There are four players below. By clicking on a player you will add a specific amount of points to your score.


You win the game by matching your total score to random numbers, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.


The value of each player is hidden from you until you click on it


Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each player
